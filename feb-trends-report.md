The Iran conflict illustrates how geopolitical instability can influence the CMF ecosystem through energy markets and logistics networks. Over time, this environment may accelerate interest in natural fibres and low-energy textile finishing techniques, reshaping both material choices and aesthetic directions in fashion, interiors, and product design.
